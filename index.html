What is a web developer?

In short, a web developer is someone whose profession is to create and make a website work, but in reality, web development is much more than that. There are three main types of web developers: front-end, back-end, and full-stack developers. First, I will talk about the most known type of developers, front-end workers. Front-end coders normally create websites using three different coding languages : HyperText Markup Language (HTML), Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), and JavaScript (JS). Front-end developers are responsible for the design of the website and they are often confused as the only people responsible for making a website. Using HTML and CSS they create all of the parts that a user sees on a website such as the buttons, text, and images and using JS, they make all of the buttons and interactive elements of a website work. Next, the back end-developers. Back-end web developers are responsible for doing all of the things that you do not see or in other words, they work on the server-side of things. Databases are often used by back-end developers as they are really important for almost all websites because they store all of the information such as the reviews on an Amazon product or the temperature on the weather app. Back-end engineers are responsible for creating the databases and the information in them as well as that, they are also managing the servers and making sure that the front-end, servers, and databases are all able to communicate with each other smoothly. Finally, there are full-stack web developers which are basically experts in both front-end and back-end therefore leading them to often get a higher salary than other web developers.

What education required for a web developer?

While it is possible to get a job in web-development without getting a degree, it is common nowadays for employers to require you to have at least a Bachelor Degree in computer science, coding, and degrees in a similar field.

What is the salary range for web developers?

Salary in web development, as in all fields, varies based on factors such as experience and employer. The type of web developer also plays a role in determining pay, with full-stack developers typically earning more on average than back-end and front-end developers. According to Xello, the average salary of a junior web developer is between $40,000 - $50,000, the average salary of an intermediate developer is between $50,000 - $80,000, and the average salary of a senior web developer/ project manager is between $70,000 - $110,000. Salaries can go even higher for really advanced coders that have many years of experience.

The future possibilities

Web development is a very future-proof job because while AI is getting better at making websites, they will never be as good as humans. Because plenty of companies are now switching to online stores, web developers have been in high demand and the demand for them will almost never go down because every day there are new companies looking for web developers to make them a website.

Information about the university I chose

The school I will be focusing on is Carleton University. Carleton University was founded about 81 years ago and it is situated in Ottawa, Ontario.
Overall, Carleton University is ranked number 526 in Best Global Universities and 20th in Best Global Universities in Canada.

Entrance Requirements

The minimum grade cut-off range (which is the academic average for admission in a program) is 85-88%. As mentioned previously, most people recommended getting a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science if aspire to be a web developer.
To enter the computer science program in Carleton University you must obviously have an Ontario Secondary School Diploma as well as at least six completed 4U or M courses with no mark below 60%. Two of the six 4U or M courses have to be Advanced Functions, and Calculus and Vectors.

Things necessary to pass the course

Students must get higher than a C- in their course or else no credits will be awarded to them. Tuition for the Computer Science program is $9,124 and other mandatory fees are $1,237 making the total cost for a full year $10,361.

Time it takes to finish the course

Like most other Bachelor Degrees, it takes 4 years to complete the program and get the degree.
